Open Dashboard
pipeline.html?rep=julia

Review Hot List
Hot leads · Going Cold alerts
Hot Leads panel shows contacts that need action today. Going Cold alerts flag leads with 7+ days of inactivity in red. Sequence status shows where each lead is in the follow-up sequence.

Make Calls / Send Emails
Execute outreach
Rep works their call list. Direct dial links open the dialer. Email links pre-fill personalized templates. RingCentral records all calls automatically.

Automated Workflows Runs behind the scenes, 24/7
▾
⏰
7 AM Pipeline Tracker
RingCentral + Outlook sync
Every morning at 7 AM: pulls RingCentral call logs and Outlook sent emails from the past 24 hours, matches them to leads in the pipeline, flags any replies received, and updates activity timestamps.
Ex: "Julia sent 3 emails yesterday — 1 reply detected — lead auto-moved to Contacted — Going Cold cleared"
🔧 In Progress
▾
📧
Follow-up Sequences
Day 1 · 4 · 8 · 14 via CEIPAL
Once a lead enters the Contacted stage, a 4-touch email sequence fires automatically via CEIPAL. Each email is personalized to the company and rep. Sequence pauses if a reply is detected.
Ex: "Day 1: Initial outreach → Day 4: Value add → Day 8: Case study → Day 14: Last try"
🔧 In Progress
▾
🌡️
Going Cold Alerts
7+ days no activity → flag red
Any lead with zero logged activity for 7+ days is automatically flagged as "Going Cold" with a red indicator. BDMs see these first in the dashboard so nothing falls through the cracks.
Ex: Lead at Day 10 with no activity → red "Going Cold" badge → appears in rep's hot list immediately
✅ Live
▾
🧠
Daily Intel Brief
AI scans → Telegram delivery
Every morning the AI scans target companies for hiring signals, funding news, and expansion announcements. A briefing is pushed to Telegram with actionable leads to add to the pipeline.
Ex: "🔥 Salesforce just posted 45 IT roles — 3 in your territory — add to pipeline? [Yes/No]"
✅ Live

Gamification Layer Activity → Points → Levels → Badges
▾

Points System
Every action earns points
Points are awarded automatically when outcomes are logged. Calls, emails, meetings, and placements all earn different point values — weighted by impact on revenue.
Call logged: +5pts · Email sent: +3pts · Meeting booked: +25pts · Placement: +100pts · New lead: +2pts
✅ Live
▾
📈
Rep Levels
Rookie → Hunter → Closer → Rainmaker
Points accumulate to unlock levels. Each level has a distinct color and badge shown on the leaderboard. Rainmaker is the top — you have to earn it.
Rookie: 0–499 · Hunter: 500–1,499 · Closer: 1,500–3,999 · Rainmaker: 4,000+
✅ Live
▾
🏅
Milestone Badges
8 badges to unlock
Badges unlock for major milestones. They display permanently on your profile and leaderboard card. Some are easy, some are hard — they tell the story of how you sell.
🎯 First Blood · 📞 Dialer · 🔥 On Fire · 💰 Closer · 🏆 Pipeline Full · ⭐ Perfect Week · 🚀 Rainmaker · 👑 Top Dog
✅ Live
▾
🏆
Leaderboard + Challenges
Weekly rankings + bonus events
Live leaderboard ranks all reps by weekly and all-time points. Weekly challenges (e.g., "Log 20 calls this week") offer bonus point multipliers — keeps the energy up all week.
Ex: "Launch Week Challenge: 20 calls = +50 bonus pts — Julia at 14/20 — Mike at 8/20"

Sales Meetings
The #1 KPI — every week
