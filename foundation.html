👤 Adding New Clients & Contacts ▼
🚨
No Exceptions

Every prospect, client, and contact goes into CEIPAL. Every single one. No exceptions.

  • Log every prospect as a client record upon first contact
  • Add all contacts (name, title, email, phone) under their company
  • Update contact records after every interaction
📅 Task Manager for BDM Meetings ▼
  • Task Creation — Log every meeting (set and completed) as a task in CEIPAL Task Manager
  • Tagging Completion — Mark tasks complete with outcomes and notes from the meeting
  • Reporting — Pull meeting reports for EOW numbers and 1:1s with Mike
⚠️
Not a spreadsheet. Not your notebook.

CEIPAL Task Manager is the only acceptable place to track meetings. Full stop.
