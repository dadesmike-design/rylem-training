🚨 Non-Negotiable Rules
  • Log EVERYTHING in CEIPAL. Every call, every email, every status update. If it's not in CEIPAL, it didn't happen.
  • RingCentral is the primary phone tool. All calls go through RC so they're recorded and reviewable.
  • Calls are scored. Screening, prep, and debriefs are evaluated against rubrics. Target: 90%+.
  • Never ghost a candidate. Even bad news delivered well builds Rylem's reputation. Candidates talk.
  • Debrief is a CALL, not a text. Schedule it during prep, conduct it within 24 hours.
  • Experience level matching is your first filter. Too senior = auto-reject. Too junior = verify hands-on experience.
  • Cross-reference CEIPAL before spending credits. We have 300,000+ candidates. Check there first.
