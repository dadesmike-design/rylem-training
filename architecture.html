Runs every morning at 7 AM. Pulls RingCentral call logs, matches calls to leads in the pipeline, flags unanswered follow-ups, and surfaces going-cold leads for the dashboard.

Sources:RingCentral API
Updates:leads.json · dashboard.json

Stages:New Leads · Contacted · Meeting Booked · Proposal Sent · Won · Lost

gamification.json
