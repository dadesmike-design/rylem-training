📋 House Rules & Rhythms

Non-negotiable operating rules and the daily/weekly/monthly cadence that keeps the machine running. These aren't suggestions.

Overview Weeks 1–2: Foundation Weeks 3–4: Execution Weeks 5–6: Acceleration House Rules Checklists Resources
🏛️ House Rules
1️⃣ CEIPAL is the Source of Truth

All sourced clients, client contacts, and meeting notes go in CEIPAL. No exceptions. If it's not in CEIPAL, it didn't happen.

2️⃣ Meetings Tracked in Task Manager Only

Meetings (set and completed) are tracked in CEIPAL Task Manager. Not a spreadsheet. Not your notebook. Not anywhere else. Task Manager.

3️⃣ RingCentral is the Primary Phone Tool

Use RingCentral for all outreach — calls and SMS. Calls are recorded and reviewable. This is how you get coaching and how Mike knows what's happening.

4️⃣ Include Mike or Lead BDM on All First-Month Meetings

Include Mike or the Lead BDM on all meetings during your first month. This is for coaching, not micromanagement. You'll get better faster.

5️⃣ Prepare EOW Numbers for Your 1:1 with Mike

Every week, before your 1:1, have these ready:

📞 Calls Made
Live dials, not voicemails
👤 Contacts Added
New client contacts in CEIPAL
📅 Meetings Set
Booked for this or next week
✅ Meetings Completed
Held this week with outcomes noted
6️⃣ EOW Numbers in CEIPAL Before Your 1:1

EOW numbers must be visible and updated in CEIPAL before your 1:1 begins. Come prepared. Do not show up to a 1:1 without your numbers ready.

📆 Weekly Rhythm
🔄 Every Week
  • L10 Meeting: Present your numbers, raise issues, commit to next week's targets
  • Pipeline Review: Review with Mike or Julia — what moved, what stalled, what's new
  • Call Review: Listen to 2 recorded calls (yours or team) for self-improvement
🗓️ Monthly Rhythm
📊 Every Month
  • Account Plan Review: Are your top 10 accounts still the right top 10? Adjust as the market changes.
  • Market Update: What's changed in your verticals? Funding news, layoffs, expansions.
  • Skill Building: One topic deep-dive — negotiation, VMS navigation, GDM pitch, etc.
