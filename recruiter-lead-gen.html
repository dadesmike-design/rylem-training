🎯 How Recruiters Generate Sales Leads

Every recruiter conversation is a lead gen opportunity. You talk to more people daily than any BDM — use it.

Overview Foundation Job Analysis Execution Acceleration Lead Gen Negotiation Objections Compliance Tech Overview Advanced Sourcing
💡 Why Recruiters Generate Leads
  • Recruiters talk to more people daily than BDMs — every call has intel
  • You're not "doing sales" — you're sharing information that helps the team win
  • Candidates tell you things they'd never tell a salesperson: who's hiring, who's growing, who just lost people
  • The best staffing companies treat every recruiter as a lead generation engine
  • This isn't extra work — it's part of the job. The intel is already in your conversations. You just need to capture it.
🔥 Lead Classification
🔴 Hot Lead
  • You've confirmed the hiring manager is ACTIVELY interviewing
  • Came from a real conversation, not a job posting
  • Must have: manager name, company, phone/email, confirmed they're hiring NOW
🟡 Warm Lead
  • You know they USE contractors/consultants, but don't know if they're actively hiring
  • Must have: manager name, company, phone/email, confirmed they use contractors
🔵 Tip / Market Intel
  • Info suggesting a company MIGHT need staffing
  • New office, expansion, layoffs at a competitor, someone mentions their company is growing
  • No confirmed hiring activity yet — but worth tracking
⏰ When Leads Happen
📞 During Candidate Screens
  • "Where else are you interviewing?"
  • "Who's your current manager?"
  • "Is your company hiring for other roles?"
📋 During Reference Checks
  • References ARE hiring managers — ask about THEIR hiring needs, not just the candidate
  • This is the most underutilized lead source in staffing
🔎 During Sourcing Research
  • You see companies posting lots of jobs? That's a lead
  • You see a company that just got funding? Lead
  • New leadership announcements, office expansions, competitor layoffs — all leads
🤝 The Handoff
📝 How to Pass Leads to BDMs
  • Log in CEIPAL immediately — don't wait until end of day
  • Include: company name, contact name, title, phone, how you got it, why it's hot/warm/tip
  • Tell the BDM directly — don't just log it and hope they find it
  • BDM follows up within 24 hours on Hot leads
⚡
Speed Matters

A hot lead that sits for 48 hours becomes a warm lead. A warm lead that sits for a week becomes worthless. Log it and tell the BDM the same day.

🚫 What NOT To Do
🚨
Know Your Lane

You are the eyes and ears, not the closer. Gather intel and pass it along.

  • Don't try to sell. You're not pitching Rylem services to the contact.
  • Don't pitch. Don't describe our capabilities, pricing, or offerings.
  • Don't promise anything. Don't tell anyone "we can help" or "someone will call."
  • Just gather intel and pass it along. Company name, contact, what you heard, why it matters.
