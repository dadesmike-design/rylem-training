  • You own the candidate relationship — the BDM owns the client relationship
  • All submissions go through the BDM to the client
  • Your job is to make the BDM look good and the candidate feel confident
  • Every interaction with a candidate represents Rylem's brand

🔎 Sourcing Fundamentals
  • Internal database first: CEIPAL has 300,000+ candidates — search there BEFORE external tools
  • Boolean search basics: AND, OR, NOT, quotes, parentheses
  • LinkedIn X-ray: site:linkedin.com/in "[title]" "[skill]" [location]
  • Job boards: CareerBuilder, Monster, Indeed
  • GitHub/StackOverflow for technical roles
  • Industry-specific boards (HitMarker for gaming, GameDevMap, etc.)
  • ALWAYS cross-reference names against CEIPAL before spending credits on external platforms
⚠️ Experience Level Matching — CRITICAL
🚨
FIRST FILTER on Every Sourcing Task

Match the JD's experience range. This is the first thing you check — before skills, before location, before anything else.

  • If JD says 2-5 years, a year or two over is fine. But 10+ years or CIO/VP/Partner = auto-reject
  • Too senior = auto-reject. They won't step back in level or comp
  • Too junior = verify hands-on domain experience (internships don't count)
  • Career trajectory matters: if someone pivoted away from the field, flag as borderline
